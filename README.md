Create your own superhero! 

Open Gemini tool on web browser. 

Type the following example prompt / use this as inspiration to create your own prompt: “Pretend you are a comic book writer. Invent a brand-new superhero whose special power is controlling sound waves. Describe what their costume looks like, what kind of personality they have, and how they first got their powers. Write it in the exciting style of a comic book!” 

Try this across other tools (ChatGPT / Perplexity / Claude / Deepseek from any web browser) and compare the results.

 Make your Storybook 

In the Gemini app, look for a menu or sidebar. Under the "Gems" section, you should see "Storybook". In the text box, enter your prompt describing the story you want to create. Be specific!  

Click on the "Add files" or a similar icon available in Gemini Storybook to upload your family photo to be used as inspiration for the characters and setting of the story.  

To use a family photo from:  

                a. Cloud Storage: Access Google Drive / One Drive etc. on web browser, sign in using your details and use the photos 

                b. Local Storage on Phone:   

                               i. Wired: Transfer photos via USB cable / SD card to local storage on laptop / desktop 

                              ii. Wireless: Transfer photos via Bluetooth / Email / Social Apps / Cloud Storage apps to local storage on laptop / desktop 

Example Prompt: " Create a magical storybook adventure inspired by this family photo. The story should be about ‘The Mehra Family,’ who love exploring new cities. Make the kids, Aarav and Meera, the main heroes who discover a hidden portal in Jaipur leading to a world of talking animals. Write the story in 4 short chapters with creative titles. Keep the language fun and easy for children, and end the story with a happy family celebration.
